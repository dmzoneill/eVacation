EMPLOYEE SECTION: Request Leave
Available to: Anyone who is a valid e-Vacation user and whose leave is recorded / tracked in e-Vacation.

Description: Allows an employee to enter and submit a leave request for approval by their manager or an alternative approver.
Note: Please ensure that you enter a comment when requesting other leaves than Annual Leave. To have more details on leaves please refer to the Circuit website:
  • Annual Leave
  • Sick Leave
  • Unpaid Leave
  • Carers Leave
  • Special Family and Emergency Leave
  • Parental Leave
  • Maternity or Adoptive Leave

To Submit a Leave Request:
  1. Select a Leave Type from the pull-down list (contact your HR dept / e-Vacation administrator if your request relates to any other type of leave).
  2. Enter a Start Date AM/PM – click on the calendar icon to enter the date and select the time (morning/afternoon) from which you will start your leave.
  3. Enter an End Date AM/PM - click on the calendar icon to enter the date and select the time (morning/afternoon) that your leave will end.
  4. (optional) Select an Alternative Approver – use this if your manager is not available to approve your leave (click on the Search icon to select a name, or enter a WWID. Note: Please ensure you keep to current practices when selecting an alternative approver.
  5. (optional) Tick the e-mail Confirmation box to receive a copy of your request via e-mail.
  6. Enter Comments (please ensure comments are entered when requesting Compassionate or Unpaid leave).
  7. Click on the Submit Leave Request button.

Once your request is submitted, e-Vacation validates the information entered, and then sends the request, via e-mail, to your manager or approver (in this instance your manager will be copied). If the information you have entered is invalid, e-Vacation displays a message (click the here link to find out the reason/s for invalid entry/entries), and highlights the incorrect field(s) in red.

Please note that if your Leave Request is across 2 consecutive years, 2 separate requests will be needed for each year.

Once a request is approved or rejected, you will receive notification via e-mail. Please do NOT reply to the e-mail account.

How does e-Vacation calculate my leave?

The number of working days within a leave request is automatically calculated.
For example:
    Start Date is 27 Apr 2001 (Friday) AM
    End Date to 30 Apr 2001 (Monday) AM

    Working days = 1.5 Days
e-Vacation knows to exclude Saturdays, Sundays and Public Holidays (see the Public Holidays page for details)

For further information about the rules used by e-Vacation, please click on the Rules link.
 
EMPLOYEE SECTION: My Leave Summary
Available to: Anyone who is a valid e-Vacation user and whose leave are recorded / tracked in e-Vacation.

Description: My Leave Summary is split into 3 different sections: Leave Summary and Leave Requests.

Leave Summary section: This provides you with a summary, for a specified year (default is the current year), of your annual leave entitlement (basic, carry-over, ELP banked for the year, the number of days of your leave entitlement which have been booked, and the balance.

The Leave Summary also summarises other types of leave (e.g. maternity), which have been booked.

An e-Vacation Administrator enters the carry-over entitlements you have and any exceptional adjustments. e.g. where an employees leave entitlements are non-standard. Please ask your e-vacation administrator if you have any doubt.

Leave Requests section: This summarises all your Leave Requests for the selected year, including the request status and the number of Working Days booked.

To see more information on a particular Leave Request:

  1. Place your mouse pointer over the blue information icon

To cancel a Leave Request:

  1. Click on the Cancel link next to the request you want to cancel
  2. Select OK or Cancel

Future or unapproved leave request will be cancelled without requiring approval. If you are cancelling a historical (approved) leave request, e-Vacation sends an e-mail to your manager/leave approver for them to approve/reject the cancellation request.
 
EMPLOYEE SECTION: ELP
Available to: Anyone who is a valid e-Vacation user or administrator

Description: A summary of your active and/or matured ELP. If you have a matured ELP, you will have the option to book your ELP.
NOTE: Follow the process described on the Circuit website to set up an Extended Leave Program.

Active ELP Section: This will be displayed if you have an active ELP. It is a summary showing, the number of dates banked per year / to be banked, the maturity date (the date from which you are able to use your ELP days) and the expiry date (the date by which you must have taken your ELP vacation).

Matured ELP Section: This will be displayed if you have a matured ELP (i.e. you have banked the required number of days to take ELP vacation). It displays the number of days banked and the date by which you must take you ELP vacation. Once today's date becomes greater than the matured ELP's expiry date, the matured ELP section will no longer be shown.

ELP Leave Request Form: This will be displayed if you have a matured ELP. It is used to book your ELP vacation.

To book an ELP vacation:

  1. From the ELP Request Form, enter the start date for your vacation.
  2. (optional) Enter the WWID of the person who you want to approve your request (if not your manager).
  3. (optional) Enter any comments.
  4. (optional) Tick the e-mail box if you wish to receive an e-mail confirming your request.
  5. Click the Submit Leave Request button.

e- Vacation will automatically work out the end date of your vacation based upon the number of days in your ELP bank.

ELP Vacation Section: This will be displayed if you have a booked ELP vacation. It shows the start and end dates, the number of days booked and request status. You also have an option to cancel the vacation request.

To cancel an ELP vacation request:

  1. From the ELP Vacation section, click on the Cancel button.
  2. Click on OK to confirm your cancellation.
MANAGER / LEAVE APPROVER SECTION: Approve Requests
Available to: Anyone who is a valid e-Vacation user, his/her manager and leave approver.

Description: A summary of all Leave Requests pending approval. As a manager you will always be able to view the requests raised by your team members.

The top section of this page will display a green message to inform you of the number of requests that require your action. The second section displays a list of approval requests. If there are no Approval Requests requiring your action, a blue message will be displayed.

To approve or reject a Leave Request

e-Vacation allows the Leave Approver to approve / reject from within e-Vacation

Once a request is approved or rejected, a confirmation e-mail is automatically generated by e-Vacation and sent to the person who requested the leave.

To approve/reject from the Approve Requests screen:

  1. Click on the View button next to the request (requests assigned to you for action will be displayed in Blue and those for your information only will be in Grey).
  2. Add any comments to the leave request panel, and click on Approve or Reject button.
MANAGER / LEAVE APPROVER SECTION: Team Leave Summary
Available to: Anyone who is a valid e-Vacation user with one or more direct-report staff.

Description: A summary of all Annual Leave for each of your direct report employees. The section Team Leave Summary is colour coded. Blue indicates that the leaves are tracked by e-Vacation (i.e. employees) and grey indicates the leaves are not tracked by e-Vacation (i.e. contractors and temps).

To view more details for each staff member:

  1. Click on the Details link next to the staff member for whom you want to view information.
  2. The screen will refresh to show you the Leave Summary for the staff member you selected.
  3. If required, click on the Team Leave Summary tab to return to the previous page.

If there is an organisation change, go through the normal process for making the change and it will automatically filter through to e-Vacation via the WDS database. If you want to change the leave tracking status of a direct report employee please raise a request with your e-Vacation Administrator.
MANAGER / LEAVE APPROVER SECTION: Delegate
Available to: Anyone who is a valid e-Vacation user with one or more direct-report staff.

Description: Allows managers to assign a delegate to approve / reject requests, or to remove a currently assigned delegate.

To activate a Delegate

  1. If you do not have a delegate set, use the Search icon to select a delegate or enter his/her WWID.
  2. Click on the Appoint Delegate button.
  3. E-Vacation will validate the WWID entry. If the WWID value is invalid a red message will be displayed (there are various reasons why this WWID may be invalid, click the here link to find out the reason).

      4. To remove the currently assigned Delegate:

      1. If you already have an active delegate set, e-Vacation displays the currently assigned active delegate's name and WWID.
      2. Click on Remove Delegate button.

      From the time you successfully assigned an active delegate, all requests raised to you as a Leave Approver will automatically be raised to your delegate for action. As a manager, you will continue to receive copies of the leave requests, plus you will still be able to approve / reject the requests.
